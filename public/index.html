About

I'm a software engineer specialising in real-time interactive simulation and game development. I'm currently a Simulation Software Engineer at RSAF's Agile Innovation Digital (RAiD AETHER), where I build high-fidelity Unreal Engine environments and training tools — from procedurally generated cities for autonomous drone training to radar and air-traffic-control teaching simulators.

I've shipped across the stack: custom C++/OpenGL game engines, Unity and Godot simulators, and full-stack web apps. I care about performance, clean architecture, and building things people genuinely use. I'm completing a BSc (Hons) in Computer Science (Real-Time Interactive Simulation) at the Singapore Institute of Technology.
